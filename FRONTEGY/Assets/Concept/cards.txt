---INFO---
P = player, E = enemy
unit attributes: HP, ATK, maxATK, DEF, maxDEF
Every round, DEF = min(DEF+1, maxDEF)
Every round, ATK = maxATK

initialMaxATK = initialHP + initialMaxDEF


---UP---
ATK = all damage
DEF DMG = damage to DEF
HP DMG = damage to HP
P/Ehit = Attacked, even when dealing 0 DEF DMG
COMMON


UNKNOWN RARITY


first: Convert(P, x) & Convert(E, x)
?: first attack against P takes no HP
Phit: Reflect(P)
Ehit: Heal(P, {HP DMG})
Pdeath, trash: Convert(E, x)

---STATUS EFFECTS---
t = target, d = duration

Heal(t, a) - Heals t with a hearts
Reflect(t) - Reflects all incoming damage for t, to sender
Convert(t, d) - Converts t to the opposing team for d rounds, including effects except itself
